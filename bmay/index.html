Buy Me
A Year

A year of freedom. A lifetime of change.

What happens when survival is no longer the barrier to ambition?

Buy Me A Year is a documentary-led social experiment providing ordinary people with an extraordinary opportunity. A year of financial freedom to explore what they choose, not what they need.

↓

The Idea

Most people don't lack ambition.

They lack time, space, and security.

We're removing that.

A select group of participants will receive a year of financial freedom, replacing the need to work, survive, or justify their time. No expectations. No guarantees. No script.

The Twist

When survival disappears, something else takes its place.

Pressure. Doubt. Possibility. And sometimes… paralysis.

What This Is

Not a

Giveaway.

Not a

Competition.

It's a

Real-world experiment.

What happens when you remove the single biggest constraint on human potential?

The Documentary

We'll follow each participant over the year.

  • The decisions they make
  • The risks they take
  • What they pursue or walk away from

Some will thrive. Some will struggle. All of it is real.
